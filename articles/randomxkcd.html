A simple XKCD Parser for random comics


Why?

I love XKCD. It's my favourite webcomic. I've bought all of Randall Munroe's books, and they sit on a proud spot on the top of my bookshelf, but often I find it annoying to click the next or random button, especially if the image changes sizes. It's just not a good format for me to read. That's why I created this.

Technical aspects

This project didn't take that long, but I ran into one monumental challenge. CORS. While XKCD does have a wonderful API, it is not CORS enabled. I either had to abandon the project or use a CORS proxy. My first choice was cors-anywhere. It's a wonderful service that allows you to bypass CORS. However, it didn't fit this projects needs. I continously got rate limited after ~1 minute of testing (about 10 requests). I don't intend to blame cors-anywhere for this, their decision for rate limits was rational. However, I needed a better service for this project. That's when I stumbled upon xkcd.now.sh. This service is a direct mirror of the XKCD API with one change: CORS was enabled. I quickly integrated it into the web app, and it worked perfectly. You can test it out below.
